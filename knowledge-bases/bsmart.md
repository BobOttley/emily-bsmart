# bSMART AI Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

### What is bSMART AI?
bSMART AI is the complete admissions ecosystem for independent schools. We provide seven integrated products — SMART Prospectus, SMART Chat, SMART Voice, SMART Phone, SMART Email, SMART CRM, and SMART Booking — that work together to make every communication intelligent and personalised.

### What makes bSMART different?
Three things: First, every product shares one database — chat, calls, prospectus views, visits all connect. Second, our AI never invents — only verified school data. Third, we're built specifically for school admissions.

### Who is bSMART for?
Independent schools — boarding schools with international recruitment, day schools in competitive markets, any school wanting to capture more enquiries and respond faster.

## SMART Prospectus

Interactive, personalised digital prospectus. Each family gets a unique link tailored to their interests. Available in 100+ languages. Emily embedded throughout. Reports which pages each family viewed.

**Features:**
- 70+ personalisation points
- Audio tours narrated by Emily
- Engagement tracking
- Automatic translation

---

## SMART Chat

Emily as a chat widget on your website. Answers parent enquiries 24/7 — nights, weekends, holidays. Captures leads and books visits automatically.

**Features:**
- 100+ languages
