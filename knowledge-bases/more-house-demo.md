# More House School - Demo Knowledge Base

## About the School

### What is More House School?
More House is an independent Catholic day school for girls aged 11-18 in Knightsbridge, London. Founded in 1953, we offer an exceptional education in a nurturing, faith-based environment.

### Location
More House School is located at 22-24 Pont Street, Knightsbridge, London SW1X 0AA. We're just a short walk from Sloane Square and Knightsbridge tube stations.

### School Type
- Independent Catholic day school for girls
- Ages 11-18 (Years 7-13)
- Non-selective entry
- Small class sizes (average 15-18 pupils)

---

## Admissions

### How do I apply?
1. Submit an enquiry form on our website or speak with Emily
2. Book a visit - open morning, private tour, or taster day
3. Complete the registration form
4. Attend an assessment day
5. Receive your offer

### Entry Points
- Year 7 (age 11) - main entry point
- Year 9 (age 13)
- Year 12 (Sixth Form, age 16)

### Open Events
We hold regular open mornings throughout the year. Book through our website or ask Emily to help you.

## Academic Life

### Curriculum
- Broad and balanced curriculum following the National Curriculum
- GCSEs in Years 10-11
- A-Levels in Sixth Form
- Small class sizes for personalised attention

### Subjects Offered
**GCSE:** English, Mathematics, Sciences, Religious Studies, Languages (French, Spanish, Italian), History, Geography, Art, Music, Drama, PE, and more.

**A-Level:** Wide range including English Literature, Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, Humanities, Arts, and Business Studies.

### Academic Results
Our girls consistently achieve excellent results, with the majority going on to their first-choice universities including Russell Group institutions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is More House selective?**
No, we are non-selective. We look for girls who will thrive in our nurturing environment.

**Do you have to be Catholic to attend?**
No, we welcome girls of all faiths and none.

**What are the class sizes?**
Average class size is 15-18 pupils, allowing for personalised attention.

**Is there a school bus?**
We don't have a school bus, but we're well-served by public transport (Sloane Square and Knightsbridge tubes).

**Can I visit the school?**
Yes! Book an open morning, private tour, or taster day. Ask Emily to help you arrange a visit.

**What support is available for learning differences?**
We have a dedicated Learning Support department to help girls with additional needs.
